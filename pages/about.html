    • About Us

    Our app was developed by two small-time indie devs and designers, Paul and Kenson, who share a passion for programming, design, and reselling. To make our tasks more fun, we decided to design an app that uses the latest AI modeling and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology to create algorithms for recognizing and pricing video games.

    The Game Pricing Data

    The data for pricing the video games is based on the industry standard, Pricecharting, and is extremely reliable for what you can expect to get after shipping and handling on sites like eBay.

    How We Use It

    At the moment, this app is free and we use it ourselves to find and buy video games. Here’s how we use it:

    • We go into thrift shops and video game shops, take photos of video games, and quickly get a price estimate.
    • Another way is by dragging and dropping photos from Facebook Marketplace into the app to quickly get a price for each game in an entire shelf or stack in seconds.

    Both methods help us make over $50 an hour, and they will for you, too! This more than makes up for the small fee that we plan to charge soon to cover our costs.
